Codel visual-novel sprite

Artwork by LisadiKaprio.
Source: https://opengameart.org/content/codel-visual-novel-sprite

Changes made for EchoSelf:
- Of the original 12 expression PNGs, six were selected and renamed to match
  EchoSelf's expression set (neutral, happy, patient, thinking, celebrating, drift).
- Each was resized from 1078x2089 to 516x1000 to keep the repository light.
No other modifications. The original PSD and full set remain at the source link above.
